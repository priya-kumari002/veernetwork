About VEER GROUP

Delivering Quality, Safety, and Innovation in Infrastructure Since 2019

VEER GROUP is a leading civil engineering and infrastructure firm specializing in road construction and tar/asphalt surfacing solutions. We combine over two decades of industry expertise with modern technology to deliver robust, reliable infrastructure projects across India.

From highways to municipal roads, and from drainage to earthwork – we’ve successfully executed projects for NHAI, State PSUs, and a variety of private clients. Our team is driven by a shared commitment to sustainability, innovation, and client satisfaction.
